Metaverse

Pioneers in Metaverse Research

Since 2017 (before metaverse was a popular word) we own the SciArt Lab District in Decentraland, one of the first blockchain-based virtual worlds.

In collaboration with worldwide companies and universities, we have developed projects to explore the potential of virtual reality for Therapy, Digital Art, Education, and Emergent Economies.

We have helped institutions to join the Metaverse and allowed brands to have a virtual presence in the space.

To join the district, contact us to contact@sciartlab.com.

Therapeutic spaces with focus on mental health.

SENSE-GARDEN is an innovative concept for care and well-being in neurocognitive disorders, using an immersive environment supported by digital technology, combining physical and virtual therapy.

There are SENSE-GARDEN centers in daily operation in Belgium, Norway, Portugal and Romania (soon in the Netherlands). You can also visit our virtual SENSE-GARDEN facilities in the Metaverse.

NFT Art

We are part of NFT Communities and projects like Bored Ape Yatch Club and World of Women. We support new collections and creators in the NFT space. We offer collaborations to emerging collectives and artists in the Metaverse. We authorize derivatives over pieces of our NFT Collection for specific projects and non-profit social campagins. We also organize virtual exhibitions in our museum in Decentraland.
