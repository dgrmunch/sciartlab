Metaverse

Pioneers in Metaverse Research

Since 2017 (before metaverse was a popular word) we own the SciArt Lab District in Decentraland, one of the first blockchain-based virtual worlds.

In collaboration with worldwide companies and universities, we have developed projects to explore the potential of virtual reality for Neuroscience Research, Mental Health, Digital Art, Education, and Emergent Economies.

We are helping institutions to join the Metaverse and allowing brands to have a virtual presence in the space. To join the district, contact us at contact@sciartlab.com or on Twitter.

Therapeutic spaces with focus on mental health.

SENSE-GARDEN is an innovative concept for care and well-being in neurocognitive disorders, using an immersive environment supported by digital technology, combining physical and virtual therapy, backed by academic research.

There are centers in daily operation in Belgium, Norway, Portugal and Romania (soon in the Netherlands).

You can also visit our virtual center in the Metaverse.

Brand

MUTANT #9514

Mutant #9514 is a NFT (Non Fungible Token) acquired by SciArt Lab in 2021, with implicit ownership over the artwork, intellectual property rights and membership to Bored Ape Yacht Club. Since then, it has become an important part of our brand, turning into something like a mascot for our organization with world-wide presence.

You can find our mutant painted in the streets of Florence, exhibited in a coffee shop in Brooklin, displayed in a DJ musical video, acting on a TV show or mentioned in a bestseller author's book. We decided to use the power of this image for pro-bono work. specially to support artists, experimental projects and social causes. We also wanted to use our mutant as an inspiration to create our own visual story.
